What we do

ID Conference is an intensely educational three-day conference for passionate practitioners of standards-based web design. If you care about code as well as content, usability as well as design, this conference you’ve been waiting for.

Our conference features 12 great speakers and sessions. For people who get up every day eager to create great user experiences online. You don’t just make websites, you make them better.

Some of Our Speakers

Eric Meyer

Eric is a 15-year veteran of the web whose thoughts and insights regarding CSS and web standards are read and heard the world over.

Jeffery Zeldman

Jeffery is one of the first designers, bloggers, and independent publishers on the web, and one of the first web design teachers.

Graphic & Web Design

Ignite your creativity, make your design skills unstoppable, sharpen your tech savvy and forge powerful connections with designers from around the globe. ID Conference is your once-a-year refresher course in resisting the daily grind to make your design, your work and your life extraordinary.

Whether the topic is creating the right content and features for your audience, designing pages that are a pleasure to read and use, or beating browsers and web gadgets into submission, our presenters are as gung-ho about it as you are. Each speaker has made genuine contributions to our industry. Each is a star
